3. Deliver `output/document.pdf` to target users.

## Payload Flow

- User opens the PDF.
- Embedded JS creates a `WScript.Shell`.
- A PowerShell command downloads and executes `backdoor.ps1`.
- The backdoor can establish persistence, exfiltrate data, or open reverse shells.

> **Note**: Works best on Windows with ActiveX support (e.g., Adobe Reader).
